Me

After my Bachelor's degree in "Journalism and Communication Studies" at the University of Zurich, I started a Master's degree in "Internet & Society". Due to the - for my taste - too dry focus on research, I dropped out after one year, in the middle of the March 2020 lockdown.

Relatively quickly, I came up with the idea of studying Web Design & Development at SAE, which I started right away six months later, parallel to my cool marketing internship, where I focused mainly on social media.

I quickly got to know and use computer languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP and frameworks such as React.js. Well-founded insights into UX and UI completed the 75% application-oriented degree programme and I immediately found a new fascination. I successfully completed the diploma programme at the beginning of April 2022 and can't wait to work in this field.

I am looking - ideally - for a junior position as a UX Designer OR/AND frontend and React developer in the Lake Zurich/Zug area from August 2022. I am hoping for a friendly, competent and motivating team in which I can (finally) be productive with my new skillset and from which I can learn a lot of new things.
